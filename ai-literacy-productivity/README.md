**# AI Literacy for Productivity**



**## Overview**



**This lab is part of the \*\*CLEAR-IT Support Operations Lab\*\*. It focuses on using Artificial Intelligence as a productivity tool in a professional remote-work scenario.**



**The exercise is based on a simulated case study involving \*\*Cabo Digital Travel\*\*, a company facing operational challenges related to client communication, task organization, repetitive administrative work, and remote workflow structure.**



**The goal of this lab is to demonstrate how AI can support professional productivity when used with clear prompts, human validation, iterative improvement, and structured workflows.**



**## Scenario**



**Cabo Digital Travel is a tourism and local experiences company that has grown through digital promotion. As demand increased, the company started facing operational issues such as delayed client responses, inconsistent digital communication, unstructured workflows, repetitive administrative tasks, and limited process documentation.**



**In this lab, I assumed the role of a remote freelancer / digital consultant helping the company improve its communication and operational workflow using AI tools strategically.**



**## Lab Objectives**



**\* Analyze a professional remote-work case study.**

**\* Identify operational challenges and priorities.**

**\* Create structured prompts for AI-assisted work.**

**\* Produce initial professional outputs.**

**\* Improve outputs through iterative prompting.**

**\* Build a structured workflow for client request management.**

**\* Reflect critically on the benefits and limitations of AI in professional work.**

**## Key Learning Points**



**This lab shows that AI is most useful when it is part of a structured professional workflow. The first AI output is often too generic, so prompting must be improved with better context, clearer objectives, specific format requirements, and quality criteria.**



**AI can improve productivity, but it does not replace human judgment. A professional still needs to validate accuracy, adapt tone, protect sensitive information, and make the final decision before delivering work to a client or team.**
